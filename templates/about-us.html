Transforming the future of finance

The Opportunity


More than half of the world's population is barred from traditional financial services, and consequently, cannot access credit to invest in their future earnings. While there has been rapid progress over the past century in transportation, communication, energy, manufacturing, or any other industry that has made significant strides, our existing financial system has not kept up since its infancy over 700 years ago. The existing system, with its predatory practices and high fees, does not work for the 2.5 billion people worldwide struggling everyday to improve their financial lives.

We're going beyond the traditional models for credit and payments, taking into account both cultural and societal shifts, by connecting the consumer cash flow directly to their desired consumption pattern. Our first product, Glass Checkout, is only the first step in our mission to help people benefit from services that will help them better manage their money, reduce risks, and build a financial identity.

At Glass, we're focused on building world-class products for those in dire need of accessible and affordable financial services, enabling access to credit and the future they deserve.
